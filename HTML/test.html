ELISAVA MASTERS GRAD SHOW EXHIBITION (2024)

The Elisava Grad Show brings together more than 125 Final Master Projects by students from several Elisava’s masters.
A group of students from around the world joined forces to challenge personal perspectives and viewpoints, engaging with tutors and mentors to make a significant impact on people, society and the planet.
This celebration of students’ final processes at Elisava offers countless stories that we can all enjoy and share at TMDC – The Matters, between 18th and 20th June.

MDEFest EXHIBITION (2024)

MDEFest 2024 is the 4th edition of the festival organized by the students in the Master in Design for Emergent Futures, celebrating the final projects of MDEF. The event took place between the 10th and the 12th of June 2024, in different locations in Barcelona. The festival activities, ranging from workshops and panels to interactive installations and exhibitions, aimed to promote future literacy, empower the collective force of creatives, and create greater consciousness about the pressing urgency of the climate and social crisis.
