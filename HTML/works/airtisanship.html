In today's rapidly evolving world, the preservation of traditional craftsmanship stands at a critical crossroads. The project aims to globalize and modernize the transmission of ancient craftsmanship knowledge through innovative AI technologies. Its primary mission is twofold: safeguarding cultural heritage and providing invaluable educational resources for future generations.

CAN A NEW EDUCATION BE POSSIBLE???

The project uses advanced machine learning and deep learning techniques to carefully study and recreate the intricate movements of skilled artisans. This technology is used to create a strong digital educational platform. It's made for art and design students, educators, and researchers, providing an immersive way to explore traditional crafts.

The initiative recognizes what modern learners and educators need. It meets the call for learning that's accessible, interesting, and forward-looking, while also preserving traditional craftsmanship. By using AI to capture and teach the detailed aspects of these crafts, the project preserves cultural heritage and encourages people to innovate with ancient knowledge in today's world.

FOUNDATIONS OF THE RESEARCH

At the heart of the project lie four foundational pillars guiding its mission: technological innovation utilizes AI to capture and teach traditional craftsmanship techniques with precision; cultural preservation ensures the safeguarding and perpetuation of artisanal skills against the threat of time; educational engagement fosters dynamic and interactive learning experiences that ignite creativity and curiosity in students and educators; global connectivity connects artisans, learners, and educators worldwide, cultivating a united community committed to preserving and advancing traditional craftsmanship for future generations.

GLOBAL KNOWLEDGE

This project is always evolving to keep up with the changing digital landscape. It's a catalyst for transforming how craftsmanship is taught, linking historical methods with modern advancements. Through its digital platform for preserving and teaching ancient skills, it not only celebrates our cultural heritage but also drives it forward with innovation. The initiative embraces "global knowledge, local production," fostering collaboration among artisans, educators, and learners worldwide. This approach ensures local traditions thrive while benefiting from global insights and technology. Moving forward, the project aims to create a harmonious balance where traditional craftsmanship thrives globally, supported by technology and a strong commitment to preserving culture and promoting quality education.
